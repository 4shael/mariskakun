<p class="has-extra-small-font-size">Maria Skakun is an abstract painter, teacher, and art therapist, and a participant in international exhibitions. The artist lives and works in France, in Paris. Was born in Belarus.</p>



<p class="has-extra-small-font-size">In 2024, the artist received an <strong>official listing from <a data-type="link" data-id="https://mariskakun.com/2024/12/12/my-works-are-now-officially-listed-on-akoun/" href="https://mariskakun.com/2024/12/12/my-works-are-now-officially-listed-on-akoun/">AKOUN</a>,</strong> the leading French art valuation expert. This prestigious recognition confirms her status in the art market and highlights the investment value of her works.</p>

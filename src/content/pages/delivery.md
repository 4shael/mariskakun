<h5 class="wp-block-heading">TRANSPORTATION</h5>



<p class="has-small-font-size">Packaging for acrylic and oil paintings on canvas stretched on a wooden frame вЂ“ cardboard box + double layer of bubble wrap.<br><br>For safe and comfortable delivery, <strong>paintings that are not stretched on a frame will be shipped rolled in a tube</strong>. This ensures better protection during transit. After delivery, the painting will need to be stretched and framed at a framing workshop. <h5 class="wp-block-heading">SHIPPING TIME</h5>



<p class="has-small-font-size">Shipping from France. Delivery is carried out within 4 working days after purchase.</p>



<h5 class="wp-block-heading">RETURNS</h5>



<p class="has-small-font-size">You can return the work back within 14 days, in which case the cost of transportation and 10% of the cost of the painting are extracted from the cost of the work. The rest of the amount will be returned to your account within 7 working days.</p>

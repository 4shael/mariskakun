<p class="has-text-align-left series-description" style="font-size:14px">“Immersive exhibition LABYRINTH BARDO” &#8211; a combination of music, painting, VJ and poetry. It is designed as a two-story maze. Six black rooms with paintings inside. The paintings are accompanied by music and poems, under which they were written. You can listen to the works through your own headphones.</p>



<p class="has-text-align-left series-description" style="font-size:14px">BARDO is a concept in Tibetan Buddhism that means “transition” or the gap between the completion of one situation and the beginning of another. The transition from one room to another symbolizes the six BARDO states: the process of dying, nonexistence, birth, life, sleep and meditative concentration.</p>

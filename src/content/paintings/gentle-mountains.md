<p>An oil painting that I painted from life in Montenegro. when the sunset began, I depicted the tenderness of the light, it gives me new ideas in art.</p>
<p>In this expressive oil piece, I&#8217;ve captured the serene and dynamic nature of the landscape, where the dance of colors and bold strokes echo the wild beauty of a maritime vista. Blending oils with pastels, I&#8217;ve sought to encapsulate the fleeting interplay of light and shadow, the gentle undulation of hills, and the vibrant spirit of the waters. Each element is a stroke of my emotion, aiming to bring a tranquil yet spirited energy to any space.</p>

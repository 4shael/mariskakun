<p>I painted these mountains from life in Monetenergo, when the sunset was just touching them.<br />
In this piece, I&#8217;ve channeled the raw, emotive power of expressionism through the vibrancy of oil and the soft texture of oil pastel. I&#8217;ve sought to capture the majestic dance of light and color across these stoic mountains, infusing each stroke with life and movement. My intention was to evoke a sense of wonder and inspiration, a reminder of nature&#8217;s boundless beauty. It&#8217;s meant to energize any space with a spectrum of serenity and passion.</p>

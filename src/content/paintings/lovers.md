<p>Eng/Fr<br />
&#8220;The lovers&#8221;<br />
The series of paintings I am currently working on &#8220;Radical Love&#8221;</p>
<p>The painting series &#8220;Radical Love&#8221; about warmth and support between people.</p>
<p>A state of Unlimited love between all sexes, what is it?</p>
<p>The line reflects the energy of the state of love.<br />
Love is like friendship, like support and honesty. Revelation of the soul and confidence</p>
<p>The painting series was formed after attending the “Boom” Music Festival in Portugal, where I felt the love of others like a hugging cloud.</p>
